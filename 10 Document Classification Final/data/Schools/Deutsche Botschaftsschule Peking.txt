'Deutsche Botschaftsschule Peking (DBP; "German Embassy School of Beijing", simplified Chinese: 北京德国使馆学校; traditional Chinese: 北京德國使館學校; pinyin: Běijīng Déguó Shǐguǎn Xuéxiào) is a German international school in Chaoyang District, Beijing.[2] It serves years 1-12.[3]
The current building, with a capacity of over 330 pupils, opened in 2004.[4]
