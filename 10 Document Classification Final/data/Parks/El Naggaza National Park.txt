El Naggaza National Park is a national park of Libya in the Murqub district close to Tripoli. It was established in 1993 and covers an area of 4,000 hectares (9,900 acres).[1]
Flora and fauna[edit]
The park hosts different kind of birds such as flamingos and gulls and also seals.
