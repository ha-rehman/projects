Shilokh Mission Hospital (Urdu: شیلوخ مشن ہسپتال) is the oldest hospital of the city Jalalpur Jattan founded by Dr Taylor in 1900s in British India.[1] It was the first hospital of the region, which started in a tent and gradually transformed into a well known hospital.[2]
At present, it has the capacity of 250 beds. The hospital is run by a christian missionary associated with the Church of Scotland. It was taken on lease by the administration of Hashmat Medical and Dental College to serve as one of its three teaching hospitals and still running by Hashmat Medical College. Anwar Rehmat is the present medical director of the hospital.[3]
