Indira Gandhi Children's hospital located in Kabul is a Children's hospital of Afghanistan.[2] It has 150 beds[3] and in 2004 started the first cerebral palsy center in Afghanistan.[4] It also has an artificial limb center opened with help of Indian government which can fit up to 1000 people with Jaipur legs[5]
Some Indian physicians working at this hospital died in the February 2010 Kabul attack conducted by Taliban.[6]
