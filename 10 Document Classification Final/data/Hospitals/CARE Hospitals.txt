CARE Hospitals is a chain of multi-specialty healthcare hospitals with 14 hospitals in 6 cities across 5 states of India .[1]

History[edit]
It was founded in 1997 by Bhupathiraju Somaraju, Chairman and Managing Director of CARE Hospitals Group. CARE Hospital is a specialty hospital for cardiac surgeons, critical care and emergency medicine specialists over 18 years.[2]
Main crew members[edit]
CARE hospitals Main crew involves "Dr.B.Soma Raju" Chairman & Managing Director, "Dr.N.Krishna Reddy" Vice-Chairman and their crew.[3][4]
